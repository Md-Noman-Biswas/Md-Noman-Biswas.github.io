Welcome to my academic portfolio!

I am **Md Noman Biswas Sibly**, a final-year Electrical and Computer Engineering student at [Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET)](https://www.ruet.ac.bd/). This site is where I share my research, projects, and notes on topics I am passionate about.

## What I Work On

My research is centered around **Trustworthy AI** — building machine learning systems that are not just accurate, but also reliable, interpretable, and safe for deployment in real-world, high-stakes settings. Practically, this means a lot of time spent on:

- Medical image classification and denoising (gallbladder ultrasound analysis)
- Vision Transformer architectures and generative models (U-Net GAN)
- Understanding where and why deep neural networks fail

## What This Blog Will Cover

I plan to use this blog to write about:

- **Research notes** — ideas, paper summaries, and lessons learned from my work
- **ML concepts** — explanations of techniques I use or find interesting
- **Competitive programming** — problem-solving patterns and contest strategies (1000+ problems solved, Pupil on Codeforces)
- **Project write-ups** — behind the scenes of my open-source projects
